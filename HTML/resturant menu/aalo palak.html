RECIPE
  1. 2-3 potatoes, cubed
  2. 11 oz (300g) spinach, chopped
  3. 1 onion, finely chopped
  4. 1 tomato, finely chopped
  5. 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  6. 1 tsp cumin seeds
  7. 1 tsp red chilli powder
  8. ½ tsp turmeric powder
  9. 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek)
  10. 2 tbsp oil/ghee
  11. Salt to taste
