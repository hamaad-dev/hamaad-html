RECIPE
  1. 1 cup Chana Dal (soaked for at least 30 mins to 1 hour)
  2. 4 tbsp Oil or Ghee
  3. 1 tsp Cumin Seeds (Zeera)
  4. 1 medium Onion (finely diced)
  5. 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  6. 2 medium Tomatoes (chopped)
  7. Spices: 1 tsp Salt (or to taste), 1 tsp Red Chili powder, ½ tsp Chili flakes, ½ tsp Turmeric, 1 tsp Coriander powder
  8. Garnish: Green chilies, coriander, ginger slices, ½ tsp Garam Masala
